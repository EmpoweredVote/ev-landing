Empowered Vote helps you find the people who represent you and understand where they stand. This page explains, in plain language, what information we collect when you use our apps and website, why we collect it, and the choices you have. If anything here is unclear, contact us at [CONTACT EMAIL].

The short version

  • You can look up your representatives without creating an account.
  • When you search by address, we send that address to a mapping service to find your districts. We don't use it to advertise to you.
  • We use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how the site is used and to fix problems.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

Information we collect

Information you give us

  • Address or location search. When you enter an address (or pick a place) to find your representatives or elections, we use it to look up the matching districts and officials.
  • Account information (optional). If you sign in or build a Compass profile, we store your sign-in details and the answers/positions you choose, so we can show how you compare with officials. Accounts are handled through our sign-in service at login.empowered.vote.
  • Messages you send us, such as feedback or bug reports, including anything you choose to include in them.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage and device data. Pages you view, taps and clicks, approximate location from your network, browser and device type, and similar technical data.
  • Product analytics, including session recordings. We use analytics tooling that can replay anonymized interactions with the interface to help us find confusing or broken experiences. [Confirm whether session replay is enabled in production, and whether inputs are masked.]
  • Cookies and local storage. Small bits of data stored in your browser to remember preferences (such as light/dark mode), keep you signed in, and support analytics. See "Cookies" below.

How we use your information

  • To find and show the representatives, candidates, and elections for your area.
  • To remember your preferences and, if you have an account, your saved location and Compass answers.
  • To operate, secure, debug, and improve our apps.
  • To respond to feedback and support requests.

Services that process data for us

We rely on a small number of trusted providers to run Empowered Vote. They process data only to provide their service to us:

  • Google Maps Platform — address autocomplete and geocoding. Your use of this feature is also subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • PostHog — product analytics and session replay.
  • Supabase — database and authentication hosting.
  • Render — website and application hosting.

Cookies & local storage

We use cookies and browser storage to:

  • Remember your theme (light/dark) and other preferences.
  • Keep you signed in across Empowered Vote apps, if you have an account.
  • Measure and improve how the site performs.

You can clear or block cookies in your browser settings; some features (like staying signed in) may not work without them.
